# 🐾 Dannie — Memorial Website

A heartfelt memorial website for **Dannie**, a beloved German Shepherd, built with love.

---

## 💙 About This Website

This memorial was created to honour and remember **Dannie** — a magnificent German Shepherd who filled every room with warmth, love, and joy. He loved mangoes, car rides, morning walks, and greeting everyone with his charming furry wagging tail.

The website is a tribute to his memory, built so that anyone who loved him can visit anytime and remember him.

---

## ✨ Features

- **Bilingual** — Full English and ಕನ್ನಡ (Kannada) support with a one-tap language toggle
- **Photo Gallery** — All 7 photos displayed cinematically with a fullscreen lightbox viewer
- **Animated Hero** — Spinning gradient ring with floating particles and glowing effects
- **Personality Traits** — Glassmorphism cards capturing who Dannie was
- **Film Strip** — Infinite scrolling cinematic photo reel
- **Candle Tribute** — Animated flickering candles with a heartfelt quote
- **Rainbow Bridge** — The classic farewell poem, beautifully styled
- **Install as App** — Can be added to Android or iOS home screen like a native app
- **Works Offline** — All photos are embedded, no internet needed after first load

---

## 📱 Install as an App (Android & iOS)

**Android:**
1. Open the website link in **Chrome**
2. Tap the **three dots menu** (top right)
3. Tap **Add to Home Screen**
4. Name it `Dannie` → Tap **Add**

**iOS (iPhone/iPad):**
1. Open the website link in **Safari**
2. Tap the **Share button** (box with arrow)
3. Tap **Add to Home Screen**
4. Name it `Dannie` → Tap **Add**

The memorial will appear on the home screen like a real app. 🐾
